Prototype One

Side navigation

The side navigation was chosen for this project based on the aesthetics of the website pictured below. Choosing a navigation bar in this format will provide users with expected logic when using devices with smaller screens - click on the burger button and you are presented with a menu pop up on the left side of your screen.

Colour

The colours used within this site consist of black, white, and shades in-between. This choice was made to make images stand out within the website. In terms of the data visualisations, this might become inportant when representing information within text - a purple bubble of 30%, for example, stands out within a site that uses mainly one colour.

Data visualisation

Despite there being no wireframe for the data visualisation at current, I have gathered ways in which I feel the data may be represented in a fun way, however, I feel that it may become cluttered and irrelevant depending on my chosen API.

Rough wireframes for the blog, design and home pages

Text

The text is not in a particular font, however, I would like to make use of a text which is both easy to read, whilst still still feeling different from the usual fonts such as Arial or Times New Roman. The navigation options make us of small case letters as text which is written in the same case (small or capital) is easier to read and makes the overall design feel more cohesive.
