Welcome to my website!

This website will be used to practise data visualisation through D3.js. It will additionally be used to post blogs, wireframes and other necessary items for interactive media 3B.

The data being collected on this website is all about food and drinks. Food and drinks make up a large part of our lives and so it become important to know what is in them. We begin by analysing the sugar content of sodas, and then the saturated fat content of crisps.
